Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Cashio ("the App") is designed to help users track income and expenses directly on their device.

Data Collection and Usage

Cashio does not collect, store, or share any personal data externally.

All data entered by the user is stored locally on the user's device only.

Notifications Permission

The app may request permission to send notifications for optional reminders created by the user.

  • Notifications are generated locally on the device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Users can disable notifications at any time

Permissions

The app may request local storage access to save user data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, ads, or third-party services.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on the device.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated in the future.
